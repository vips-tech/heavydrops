## 🎯 Design Elements

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary Text:** Dark gray (#2A2A2A)
- **Headings:** Black (#0A0A0A)
- **Accents:** Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Background:** White with gold tints
- **Hero:** Dark gradient with overlay

### Typography:
- **Font Family:** Inter (body), Outfit (headings)
- **Body Size:** 1.05rem
- **Line Height:** 1.8 (excellent readability)
- **Heading Sizes:** Responsive with clamp()

### Spacing:
- **Section Margins:** 3-4rem
- **Paragraph Spacing:** 1.5rem
- **Card Padding:** 2rem
- **Content Max Width:** 900px

---

## 📱 Responsive Design

### Desktop (>768px):
- Full hero height (40vh)
- Wide content area (900px max)
- Generous padding (5rem vertical)
- Large typography

### Mobile (<768px):
- Reduced hero height (30vh)
- Compact padding (3rem vertical)
- Smaller card padding (1.5rem)
- Responsive typography
