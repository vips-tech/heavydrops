Wallet & Credits Policy

Non-Withdrawable Nature

The Heavy Drops Wallet is an **Intent-Specific Ledger**. Credits held within this wallet are non-refundable and non-withdrawable into bank accounts or external cash equivalents.

1. Purpose of Credits

Wallet balances are used exclusively for platform-gated actions, including:

  • Triggering intent blocks for specific designs.
  • Maintaining a 'Proof of Seriousness' balance for high-value discoveries.
  • Settlement of behavioral penalties (e.g., No-Show fines).

2. No Cash Equivalence

The credits held in the Heavy Drops wallet are not "currency" but represent pre-paid access to the platform's high-intent infrastructure. They cannot be used to purchase jewellery directly; jewellery purchases must be completed at the seller's showroom using their accepted payment methods.

3. Expiry & Forfeiture

Credits may expire if the account remains inactive for a period exceeding 12 months. Any credits deducted as penalties for violating trust protocols (Strike system) are forfeited to support platform governance costs.
