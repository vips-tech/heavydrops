Wallet & Credits Policy

Understanding how your Heavy Drops wallet works and credit management

Non-Withdrawable Nature

The Heavy Drops Wallet is an Intent-Specific Ledger. Credits held within this wallet are non-refundable and non-withdrawable into bank accounts or external cash equivalents.

1. Purpose of Credits

Wallet balances are used exclusively for platform-gated actions, including:

  • Triggering intent blocks for specific designs
  • Maintaining a 'Proof of Seriousness' balance for high-value discoveries
  • Settlement of behavioral penalties (e.g., No-Show fines)
  • Platform service fees and reservation charges

2. No Cash Equivalence

The credits held in the Heavy Drops wallet are not "currency" but represent pre-paid access to the platform's high-intent infrastructure.

Important: Credits cannot be used to purchase jewellery directly. Jewellery purchases must be completed at the seller's showroom using their accepted payment methods.

3. Credit Usage

Your wallet credits can be used for:

  • Intent Signals: Reserve designs for 48-hour viewing windows
  • Appointment Booking: Secure time slots with verified sellers
  • Platform Services: Access premium discovery features
  • Penalty Settlement: Resolve no-show or cancellation fees

4. Adding Credits

Credits can be added to your wallet through:

  • Direct bank transfer or UPI payment
  • Credit/Debit card payments
  • Net banking
  • Digital wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay)

All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways with industry-standard encryption.

5. Expiry & Forfeiture

Credits may expire if the account remains inactive for a period exceeding 12 months.

Any credits deducted as penalties for violating trust protocols (Strike system) are forfeited to support platform governance costs.

Expiry Notifications

  • 90 days before expiry: First notification
  • 30 days before expiry: Second notification
  • 7 days before expiry: Final warning

6. Refund Policy

Credits are generally non-refundable. However, refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Technical errors resulting in duplicate charges
  • Unauthorized transactions (subject to investigation)
  • Platform service failures preventing credit usage

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction with supporting documentation.

7. Account Closure

Upon account closure:

  • Unused credits will be forfeited after 30 days
  • Pending intent signals will be cancelled
  • No refunds will be issued for remaining balance
  • Transaction history will be retained for legal compliance

8. Security & Protection

We implement multiple security measures to protect your wallet:

  • Two-factor authentication for transactions above ₹5,000
  • Transaction notifications via SMS and email
  • Spending limits and velocity checks
  • Fraud detection and prevention systems

9. Contact for Wallet Issues

For wallet-related queries or disputes:

  • Support Hours: Monday-Saturday, 10 AM - 6 PM IST
