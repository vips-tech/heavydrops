1. Platform Role & Identity

Heavy Drops is a high-intent discovery and appointment infrastructure. We provide the digital tools for buyers to signal intent and schedule physical showroom visits.

IMPORTANT: Heavy Drops is NOT a jewellery seller, reseller, or retailer. We do not take title to any jewellery, nor do we act as a guarantor for any transaction, product quality, or purity.

2. Seller Responsibilities

All jewellery designs displayed on the platform are provided by third-party verified sellers ("Jewellers"). The Seller is solely responsible for:

  • Accuracy of product information (Weight, Category, Purity).
  • Adherence to live gold rate snapshots.
  • Physical product quality and authenticity.
  • Completion of the sales transaction during the store visit.
  • Providing proper certification and documentation for all jewelry pieces.
  • Maintaining professional standards during customer appointments.

3. Appointment Infrastructure

Appointments booked via Heavy Drops are intended to facilitate physical discovery. The platform is not liable for seller performance, no-shows, or the outcome of physical visits.

Physical Verification Required

Physical try-ons are required before any purchase. Digital "try-ons" are visual aids only and do not represent actual product appearance, fit, or quality.

Appointment Policies

  • Appointments must be confirmed by both parties
  • Cancellations should be made at least 24 hours in advance
  • No-shows may result in account restrictions
  • Rescheduling is subject to seller availability

4. Intent Signals

Signaling intent (blocking a design) involves a non-withdrawable service fee. This fee is for the reservation service and does not constitute a partial payment for the jewellery itself.

Service Fee Details

  • Service fees are non-refundable once intent is signaled
  • Fees cover platform infrastructure and seller coordination
  • Intent signals are valid for 48 hours unless extended
  • Multiple intent signals may be subject to additional verification

5. User Obligations

By using Heavy Drops, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information during registration
  • Maintain the confidentiality of your account credentials
  • Use the platform only for legitimate jewelry discovery purposes
  • Respect seller time and honor confirmed appointments
  • Conduct yourself professionally during all interactions
  • Not misuse the intent signaling system

Prohibited Activities

Users must not:

  • Create multiple accounts to circumvent platform limits
  • Share account access with unauthorized parties
  • Attempt to contact sellers outside the platform before appointments
  • Use the platform for any illegal or fraudulent purposes
  • Scrape, copy, or redistribute platform content without permission

6. Privacy & Security

Data Collection

We collect and process personal information necessary to provide our services, including:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Appointment preferences and history
  • Design likes and browsing behavior
  • Transaction-related data for intent signals

Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Facilitate appointments between buyers and sellers
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Send relevant notifications about appointments and designs
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Pricing & Transparency

All prices displayed on Heavy Drops are estimates based on:

  • Live Gold Rates: Updated regularly from market sources
  • Weight: As provided by the seller
  • Making Charges: Set by individual sellers
  • GST: Calculated as per applicable tax rates

Final prices are determined during the physical appointment and may vary based on actual weight verification and current market rates.
